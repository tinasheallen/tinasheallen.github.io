UI Design for Alpharose E-commerce Clothing Platform

House of Alpharose, an upscale fashion brand, seeks to transition from physical stores to online retailing.

Let's connect

Objective

The objective of this case study is to address the challenges faced by House of Alpharose, an upmarket fashion brand, as it transitions from brick and mortar stores to online selling. The key objectives are to redesign their e-commerce website and create a new mobile app that aligns with their luxury image and provides a seamless, user-friendly shopping experience for customers. The case study aims to identify the existing problems with the current website and propose solutions through a comprehensive UX design process, including research, analysis, ideation, wireframing, and high-fidelity design. The ultimate goal is to create an online platform that not only showcases the brand's upscale fashion products but also enhances customer engagement, conversion rates, and brand loyalty through improved user experience and visual appeal.

Process process process! And impact over output! Companies care more about knowing that you can apply the UX process to their problems than they do about the aesthetic you had in previous roles.
